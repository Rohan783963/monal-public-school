About Us

Monal Public School is a leading educational institution dedicated to fostering a nurturing and innovative learning environment. Established in 1995, our school has consistently provided quality education and holistic development to students from diverse backgrounds.

Our mission is to equip students with the skills and knowledge needed to succeed in an ever-changing world while promoting ethical values and a sense of community.

Academics

At Monal Public School, we offer a comprehensive curriculum that includes core subjects such as Mathematics, Science, and Language Arts, as well as a variety of extracurricular activities. Our dedicated faculty members employ innovative teaching methods to ensure that each student reaches their full potential.

We are committed to academic excellence and provide a range of resources and support to help our students thrive.
